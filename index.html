Common Health Conditions

Diabetes

Symptoms:

Frequent urination, excessive thirst, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, blurred vision

Treatment:

Blood sugar monitoring, insulin therapy, healthy diet, regular exercise, medication as prescribed

Migraine

Symptoms:

Severe headache, nausea, vomiting, sensitivity to light and sound, visual disturbances

Treatment:

Pain relievers, triptans, rest in dark quiet room, stress management, preventive medications

Asthma

Symptoms:

Shortness of breath, wheezing, chest tightness, coughing, especially at night or early morning

Treatment:

Bronchodilators, inhaled corticosteroids, avoiding triggers, peak flow monitoring, action plan

Influenza (Flu)

Symptoms:

Fever, body aches, fatigue, cough, sore throat, runny nose, headache

Treatment:

Rest, fluids, antiviral medications if prescribed, pain relievers, throat lozenges

Anemia

Symptoms:

Fatigue, weakness, pale skin, shortness of breath, dizziness, cold hands and feet

Treatment:

Iron supplements, vitamin B12, folate, dietary changes, treating underlying causes

COVID-19

Symptoms:

Fever, cough, shortness of breath, loss of taste/smell, fatigue, body aches

Treatment:

Rest, isolation, fluids, symptom management, medical care if severe, vaccination prevention

UTI (Urinary Tract Infection)

Symptoms:

Burning sensation when urinating, frequent urination, cloudy urine, pelvic pain

Treatment:

Antibiotics, increased fluid intake, cranberry juice, proper hygiene, pain relievers

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Symptoms:

Often no symptoms, headaches, dizziness, nosebleeds, shortness of breath

Treatment:

Lifestyle changes, low-sodium diet, exercise, weight management, blood pressure medications

Depression

Symptoms:

Persistent sadness, loss of interest, fatigue, sleep changes, appetite changes, hopelessness

Treatment:

Therapy, antidepressants, lifestyle changes, support groups, stress management, exercise

Allergies

Symptoms:

Sneezing, runny nose, itchy eyes, skin rash, swelling, difficulty breathing

Treatment:

Antihistamines, avoiding allergens, nasal sprays, allergy shots, emergency epinephrine
